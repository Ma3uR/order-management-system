# AI Features for Order Management System - Simple Overview

## 🟢 **SIMPLE FEATURES** (1-3 days)

### 1. **Product Name Standardization** ⭐
Clean up product names from different marketplaces into consistent format for better inventory management and reporting.

### 2. **Customer Sentiment Analysis** ⭐ 
Analyze Rozetka customer ratings and order comments to identify happy/unhappy customers and predict potential issues.

### 3. **Smart Customer Risk Scoring** 🆕
Calculate risk score for customers based on order history, ratings, and patterns across all marketplaces to prevent fraud.

### 4. **Order Anomaly Detection** 🆕
Automatically detect unusual order patterns (suspicious timing, addresses, quantities) that might indicate problems.

---

## 🟡 **MODERATE FEATURES** (4-7 days)

### 5. **Dynamic Pricing with Web Scraping** ⭐
Use Puppeteer to scrape competitor prices from OLX, Rozetka, Epicentr, Prom and suggest optimal pricing.

### 6. **Marketplace Optimization Engine** 🆕
Analyze which marketplace works best for each product and recommend where to focus sales efforts.

### 7. **Automated Customer Support** ⭐
AI chatbot that answers common customer questions using order data and marketplace information.

### 8. **Product Performance Intelligence** 🆕
Track how products perform across different marketplaces and identify trends, bestsellers, and underperformers.

---

## 🔴 **COMPLEX FEATURES** (1-3 weeks)

### 9. **Advanced Analytics Dashboard** ⭐
Comprehensive dashboard with sales predictions, trends, and business insights across all marketplaces.

### 10. **Voice-to-Expense Processing** ⭐
Record expenses by voice, AI automatically categorizes and enters them into the system.

### 11. **Market Intelligence Engine** 🆕
Advanced web scraping system that monitors competitor prices, stock levels, and market trends across all platforms.

### 12. **Cross-Platform Customer Intelligence** 🆕
Create unified customer profiles by combining data from all marketplaces to understand buying behavior.

---

## 🤖 **NON-AI AUTOMATION** (2-4 days each)

### 13. **Rule-Based Order Processing**
Automatically approve trusted customers, route orders by region, assign priorities based on order value.

### 14. **Smart Notification System**
Automatically send delivery confirmations, payment reminders, and follow-up messages based on order status.

### 15. **Marketplace Sync Optimization**
Optimize when and how often to sync data from each marketplace for better performance and fewer errors.
